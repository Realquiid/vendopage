The Problem We Solve

Vendors were losing sales every 24 hours.

Across the world — in Lagos, Nairobi, Manila, São Paulo, Dhaka, and beyond — millions of small business owners run their shops through WhatsApp and Instagram. They photograph their products, post them to their status, and watch them disappear a day later. Customers scroll back and can't find that dress they saw yesterday. Orders are lost. Revenue is lost. Time is wasted re-uploading the same products every single day.

Our Answer

A permanent store that lives at your own link.

VendoPage gives every vendor — regardless of country, language, or technical skill — a professional storefront they can set up in 30 seconds. Upload your photos, add your prices, and share one link everywhere. Your customers can always find your products. Your sales never disappear.

How We Got Here

Our journey.

From a simple observation to a platform serving vendors worldwide.

The Observation
A vendor, a problem.
We watched vendors spend hours every day re-uploading the same products to their WhatsApp status — only to have them disappear in 24 hours. Customers couldn't find products they saw the day before. Sales evaporated. The problem was clear.
The Build
Built for real workflows.
We didn't build a generic ecommerce platform. We built specifically for how vendors actually work — phone cameras, WhatsApp screenshots, casual descriptions, and direct customer conversations. No complicated dashboards. No merchant accounts. Just a store that works.
The Launch
Vendors go live worldwide.
VendoPage launched and vendors from multiple continents signed up within the first days. Fashion sellers in West Africa, food vendors in Southeast Asia, electronics dealers in South America — the problem we solved was universal.
The Future
Scaling global social commerce.
We're expanding features, languages, and payment integrations to serve the growing global market of mobile-first vendors. VendoPage is becoming the infrastructure layer for social commerce worldwide.
