Built-in Protection

How escrow keeps everyone safe.

Buying from a stranger online is scary. Escrow removes that fear — for both sides.

Vendopage Escrow

Money held by Vendopage until both sides are satisfied

Buyer pays at checkout

Payment secured via Paystack. Money goes into escrow — not to the seller yet.

Vendopage holds the funds

Money sits safely with Vendopage. Seller sees the order but hasn't received anything yet.

3

Seller ships and uploads tracking

Seller ships the order and uploads courier tracking or driver's contact number to the dashboard.

4

Buyer receives tracking by email

Vendopage automatically emails the buyer their tracking details so they can follow the delivery.

5

Buyer confirms delivery (or 72hr auto-release)

Once the buyer confirms receipt, funds are released. If they don't respond in 72 hours, funds are released automatically to the seller.

6

Funds hit seller's bank account

Payment lands directly in the seller's verified bank account. If there's a dispute, our team reviews within 48 hours and issues a fair resolution.
