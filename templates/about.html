The Problem We Solve

Vendors were losing sales every 24 hours.

WhatsApp Status posts disappear in 24 hours. Customers scroll back and can't find that dress they saw yesterday. The same products get re-uploaded every single day — and orders get lost along the way.

Buyers, meanwhile, were afraid to send money to strangers. Sellers got ghosted after delivery. Disputes had nowhere to go.

Vendopage fixes both sides. One permanent link for vendors. One safe payment system for buyers.
