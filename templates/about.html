About VendoPage

Making online selling simple for everyone

Our Story

VendoPage was created to solve a simple problem: sellers were losing sales because their WhatsApp stories expired after 24 hours.

Small business owners spent hours reposting products daily, only to watch them disappear. Customers couldn't find items they saw yesterday, and sales were lost.

Our Mission

We help small businesses showcase their products permanently, making it easier for customers to discover and buy.

What We Do

VendoPage gives you a permanent catalog. No more daily uploads, no more lost sales. Just one simple link to share everywhere.
