Payments & Store Mode

Vendopage operates in two modes:

Free Catalogue Mode

In Catalogue Mode, buyers contact vendors directly via WhatsApp to complete purchases. Vendopage is not involved in these transactions — payment, delivery, and any disputes are handled entirely between the vendor and buyer. Vendopage takes no commission and processes no payments in this mode.

Store Mode (direct payments)

In Store Mode, vendors accept direct payments on their Vendopage store page. Payments are processed securely via Flutterwave. Vendopage holds funds in escrow until delivery is confirmed. To enable Store Mode, you must first add and verify a Nigerian bank account in your dashboard Settings. By enabling Store Mode, you agree to:

  • Providing a verified Nigerian bank account for payouts

Escrow Service

When a buyer pays via escrow in Store Mode, the following applies:

  • Funds held: Payment is received by Vendopage and held in escrow — not transferred to the seller — until delivery is confirmed or the 72-hour auto-release period expires.
  • Seller obligation: The seller must ship the order and upload a valid courier tracking number or driver's contact number to the dashboard within a reasonable time.
  • Buyer confirmation: Once the buyer confirms receipt, funds are released. Payout is processed via our daily cron at 2 AM the night after confirmation, after a 24-hour buffer to allow for disputes.
  • 72-hour auto-release: If the buyer does not confirm or dispute within 72 hours of the seller marking the order shipped, the order moves to RECEIVED status and is queued for the next payout run.
  • T+1 payout cycle: Payouts are processed nightly in the order received (oldest first), subject to Flutterwave's T+1 clearing cycle. Sellers are paid in chronological order.
  • Direct payment option: Buyers may also choose to pay the seller directly (outside escrow). In this case, Vendopage holds no funds and is not responsible for the outcome of the transaction.
Escrow only covers payments made through Vendopage

Escrow protection only applies to payments made directly on the Vendopage platform using Store Mode. Payments made via bank transfer, mobile money, or any method outside the platform are not covered.

Disputes & Refunds

If a buyer has not received their order, or the item received does not match the listing, they may open a dispute before confirming delivery. Vendopage will:

  • Review the dispute within 48 hours
  • Request evidence from both parties (photos, tracking records, messages)
  • Issue a fair resolution — either releasing funds to the seller or refunding the buyer via Flutterwave

Because funds are held in escrow, the buyer's money has not been sent to the seller at the time of a dispute. This means refunds can be issued promptly when the buyer's claim is valid. Refunds are processed via Flutterwave and typically arrive within 3–5 business days.

Disputes must be raised before confirming delivery

Once a buyer confirms delivery, the transaction is considered complete and funds are queued for payout to the seller. Disputes cannot be raised after this point through Vendopage. Please inspect your order carefully before confirming receipt.
