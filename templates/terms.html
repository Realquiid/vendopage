Payments & Store Mode

Vendopage operates in two modes:

Free Catalogue Mode

In Catalogue Mode, buyers contact vendors directly via WhatsApp to complete purchases. Vendopage is not involved in these transactions — payment, delivery, and any disputes are handled entirely between the vendor and buyer. Vendopage takes no commission and processes no payments in this mode.

Store Mode (paid payments)

In Store Mode, vendors can accept direct payments on their Vendopage store. Payments are processed securely via Paystack. Vendopage acts as an intermediary holding funds in escrow until delivery is confirmed. By enabling Store Mode, you agree to:

  • Providing a verified Nigerian bank account for payouts
  • Paystack's terms of service for payment processing
  • Vendopage's escrow terms as described below
  • Any applicable transaction or service fees as communicated in your dashboard

Escrow Service

When a buyer pays via escrow in Store Mode, the following applies:

  • Funds held: Payment is received by Vendopage and held in escrow — not transferred to the seller — until delivery is confirmed or the 72-hour auto-release period expires.
  • Seller obligation: The seller must ship the order and upload a valid courier tracking number or driver's contact number to the dashboard within a reasonable time.
  • Buyer confirmation: Once the buyer receives and confirms their order, funds are released to the seller's verified bank account.
  • 72-hour auto-release: If the buyer does not respond within 72 hours of the seller marking the order as delivered, funds are automatically released to the seller.
  • Direct payment option: Buyers may also choose to pay the seller directly (outside escrow). In this case, Vendopage holds no funds and is not responsible for the outcome of the transaction.
Escrow only covers payments made through Vendopage

Escrow protection only applies to payments made directly on the Vendopage platform using Store Mode. Payments made via bank transfer, mobile money, or any method outside the platform are not covered by Vendopage escrow.

Disputes & Refunds

If a buyer has not received their order, or the item received does not match the listing, they may open a dispute before confirming delivery. Vendopage will:

  • Review the dispute within 48 hours
  • Request evidence from both parties (photos, tracking records, messages)
  • Issue a fair resolution — either releasing funds to the seller or refunding the buyer

Because funds are held in escrow, the buyer's money has not been sent to the seller at the time of a dispute. This means refunds can be issued promptly when the buyer's claim is valid.

Disputes must be raised before confirming delivery

Once a buyer confirms delivery, the transaction is considered complete and funds are released to the seller. Disputes cannot be raised after this point through Vendopage. Please inspect your order before confirming.
