Questions

💳 Do I need to pay to accept payments in my store?

No. Store Mode (escrow payments) is completely free to enable. We take {{ transaction_fee|floatformat:"-2" }}% from each sale — only when you make money.

🔒 How does the escrow work?

When a buyer pays, the money is held securely. After you ship and the buyer confirms receipt (or 72 hours pass with no dispute), the money is transferred to your bank account automatically.

⭐ What does Premium actually add?

Premium removes the "Powered by Vendopage" badge from your store, lets you disable watermarks on product images, gives you homepage placement, and priority support. Your store looks 100% like your brand.

🔄 Can I cancel Premium anytime?

Yes. Your premium features stay active until the end of your billing period. No penalties, no questions asked.

📦 What happens to my products if I downgrade?

Nothing disappears. All your products stay live. The "Powered by Vendopage" badge returns, and watermarks go back on — that's all.

🔒 Is payment secure?

All payments go through Flutterwave, a PCI-DSS certified processor. We never store your card details or your buyers' card details.
