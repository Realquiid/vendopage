Overview

VendoPage is a global platform that helps vendors in every country create and share their product catalogues. When you use VendoPage, you trust us with your information. We take that seriously.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of VendoPage worldwide — including vendors who create stores, visitors who browse them, and customers who interact with vendor listings.

Our core commitment

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, use it for advertising unrelated to VendoPage, or share it without a legitimate reason. Your data powers your store — nothing else.

What We Collect

We collect two types of data: information you give us directly, and information that's collected automatically when you use the platform.

Information you provide

Account info
Email, business name, phone number, store URL, account password
Store content
Product photos, descriptions, prices, business category, profile picture
Communications
Messages you send us via the contact form or support email

Automatically collected

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
  • Device info — browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Network info — IP address, approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Store analytics — how many times your store and products have been viewed

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government ID numbers, financial account details, or biometric information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to operate and improve VendoPage. Specifically:

  • To run your store — displaying your products, generating your store URL, and making your catalogue visible to customers
  • To communicate with you — sending account-related emails, security alerts, and product updates (you can unsubscribe from non-essential communications)
  • To improve the platform — understanding how vendors and visitors use VendoPage helps us build better features
  • To prevent fraud and abuse — detecting suspicious activity and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • To provide analytics to you — showing you how many people viewed your store and products

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements. We do not build advertising profiles from your activity.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

Publicly visible by design

When you create a store on VendoPage, your business name, store URL, product listings, and profile picture are publicly visible — that's the whole point of the platform. You control what goes on your store page.

Service providers

We work with trusted third-party providers to operate VendoPage — including cloud hosting, email delivery, and analytics services. These providers are contractually bound to only use your data to provide services to us and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of VendoPage, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

If VendoPage is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before this happens and you will have the option to delete your account.

Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard protections including encryption at rest and in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security audits.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate security purposes.

No system is 100% secure

We implement strong security practices, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@vendopage.com.

Cookies

VendoPage uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function (login sessions, security tokens). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how vendors and visitors use VendoPage, so we can improve it. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some platform functionality.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have rights over your personal data. These include:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — you can update your account information at any time from your dashboard
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data from your account settings
  • Portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Objection — you can object to certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@vendopage.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or other regions with specific data protection laws (including GDPR, NDPA, PDPA, LGPD, and others), you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children's Privacy

VendoPage is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, we will delete that data promptly.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us at support@vendopage.com.

International Users

VendoPage is a global platform serving vendors in every country. By using VendoPage, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, where data protection laws may differ.

We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected regardless of where it is processed, consistent with applicable laws in your region.

We are actively working toward compliance with regional data protection frameworks including GDPR (EU/UK), NDPA (Nigeria), PDPA (Thailand/Philippines variants), LGPD (Brazil), and others as our platform grows.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email and update the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of VendoPage after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vendopage.com
  • Contact form: vendopage.com/contact

We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 30 days.
